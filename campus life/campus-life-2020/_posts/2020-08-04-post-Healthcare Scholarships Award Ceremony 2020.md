The **Healthcare Scholarship Award Ceremony 2020** was held virtually on 29 July 2020, graced by Guest-of-Honour Mr Chan Yeng Kit, Permanent Secretary (Health), Ministry of Health. We would like to extend our sincere congratulations to our graduands who received awards at the ceremony. As they embark on their journey as healthcare scholars, they will be playing an important role in shaping the future of Singapore’s public healthcare, and making a positive difference to people’s lives and their family.
