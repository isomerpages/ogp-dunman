The Chinese New Year Celebrations was held on 31 January 2020. This day was specially chosen as it was the seventh day of the first month of the lunar calendar, which is traditionally considered the birthday of everyone. Moreover, it was the day where we welcome back all our Year 5 students from their work attachment!  
One highlight of this year’s programme is the #一人一道年夜菜 Instagram contest, looking at the close to 200 entries submitted, the contest has indeed given Dunmanians an opportunity to learn more about their families’ favourite dish, revisit some traditions we have with our family, and appreciate our parents or grandparents for their loving efforts each new year.

The celebrations commenced with a lion dance performance followed by the mass greetings segment where Dunmanians sent well wishes to the school. Students then then proceeded to the Hall and Performing Arts Centre for the concerts and enjoyed performances put up by their schoolmates from the Chinese Orchestra, Wushu, Chinese Dance and Crosstalk.

All in all, it was a joyous celebration and a wonderful way to usher in the year of the Rat.

德明政府中学于2020年1月31日隆重庆祝农历新年。从一月中开始，整个校园就张灯结彩，全校弥漫着新年的气息。今年，双文化理事会同语特理事会在庆祝会前举办了“#一年一道年夜菜”的社交媒体比赛，吸引了将近200位师生投稿。从中，可以看出学生们对他们年夜菜有更深入的了解，也可以借此机会向辛苦准备年夜菜的家人们表达感激之情。

在团拜当天，在德明醒狮队的锣鼓声中，全校2600多名师生，连同校长、副校长、退休校长、学校咨询委员会代表、校友会代表以及家长支援会代表齐聚正心园，进行新春大团拜，场面壮观，热闹非凡。德明大家庭的成员们在绿意盎然的正心园，拱起手来，大声喊出祝福之语，期许大家在新的一年里万事如意，心想事成。接着，全校师生先分别在礼堂与表演艺术中心欣赏了富有华族文化气息的文娱表演。

让我们在新的一年里，恭祝大家万事如意，有“鼠”不尽的欢乐、健康与财富！
