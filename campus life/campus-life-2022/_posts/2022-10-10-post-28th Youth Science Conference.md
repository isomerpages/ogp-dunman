The **28th Youth Science Conference (YSC)** was organised by the MOE Gifted Education Branch, and was held virtually on 16 September 2022. The conference showcased 47 Science Mentorship Programme (SMP) and 70 School Based SMP projects this year.

Under the guidance of their teacher mentors and our Molecular Lab Consultant, Ms Josephine Neo, all 9 Year 3 SMP teams qualified to participate and present their research projects for the conference. After nine long months of hard work, tenacity, and grit, we are pleased to share that our teams received the following recognition from the organisers:
