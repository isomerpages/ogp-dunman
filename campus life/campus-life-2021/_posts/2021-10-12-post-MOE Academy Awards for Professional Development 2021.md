This year, the Academy of Singapore Teachers (AST) received 409 unique nominations for the Academy Awards 2021 for contributions to professional development. We are pleased to share that the final award recipients include 2 of our teaching staff! Mdm Khoo Geok Hwa, Lead Teacher/High Ability Learners who teaches Math at the Junior High level, received the Associate of AST Award in the area of facilitation in Networked Learning Communities (NLC). Mdm Ho Su Siew, HOD/ICT, who teaches Chinese at the Junior High level, received the Associate of AST Award in the area of innovation.
