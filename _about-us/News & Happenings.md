<h3><strong>2023 GCE ‘A’ Level Results&nbsp; </strong><br><strong>A 水准考试成绩</strong></h3>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Homepage/senior%20high.png">
</div>
<p>The school congratulates the 2023 Year 6 cohort for their excellent achievements
in the 2023 GCE A-Level Examinations! A total of 436 Dunmanians sat for
the Examinations, and they have done themselves and the school proud with
their hard work and achievements!</p>
<p></p>
<p>The cohort achieved a median University Admission Score (UAS) of 83. More
than 1 in 5 students scored at least 6 distinctions. Apart from doing well
in the H1 and H2 subjects, our students have achieved highly commendable
results in the H3 subjects with about 4 in 5 students who offer the H3
subjects scored a Distinction or Merit.</p>
<p></p>
<p>With our Dunmanians’ excellent academic performance and impressive achievements
in the CCAs, they have proven themselves to be well-equipped to care, to
serve and to lead, and are ready to contribute to Singapore in their capacities
as Role-Models of Learning, Leaders of Honour and Pillars of Society.</p>
