<h2><strong>Staff Values: SPRIDE</strong></h2>
<h3><strong>教员成果</strong></h3>
<h4>Synergy &nbsp;协同效应</h4>
<p>We believe the “whole is greater than the sum of its parts!” – Together
Everyone can Achieve More.</p>
<h4>Professionalism &nbsp;专业操守</h4>
<p>We uphold our teaching profession with pride.</p>
<h4>Respect &nbsp;尊重彼此</h4>
<p>We value diversity as everyone is unique &amp; we treat one another with
dignity.</p>
<h4>Integrity &nbsp;诚信为本</h4>
<p>We are consistent in our thoughts, words and deeds in accordance with
strong moral principles.</p>
<h4>Dedication &nbsp;尽心尽意</h4>
<p>We are passionate and committed to the development of the students and
the organisation.</p>
<h4>Excellence &nbsp;精益求精</h4>
<p>We build a culture of improvement and innovation.</p>
