<p>For 2026, the JAE appeal period is from: <strong>3 Feb 2026, 0900h to 9 Feb 2026, 1200h</strong>.</p>
<p>Only applicants who meet the cut-off points (COP) of DHS for JAE 2026
will be considered for&nbsp;appeals.&nbsp;
<br>
<br>All&nbsp;appeal&nbsp;applications&nbsp;must be&nbsp;<strong>completed&nbsp;online</strong>&nbsp;through
FormSG here:&nbsp;<strong><a href="https://go.gov.sg/dhsjaeappeal2026" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/dhsjaeappeal2026</a></strong>,
from&nbsp;<strong>3 Feb 2026, 0900h</strong>.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The deadline for submission of the&nbsp;online&nbsp;appeal&nbsp;application&nbsp;is<strong>&nbsp;9 Feb 2026, 1200h</strong>.
&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Late or incomplete submissions will not be considered</strong>.</p>
<p>Applicants are required to&nbsp;upload&nbsp;the following relevant documents
through FormSG</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Dunman High&nbsp;JAE&nbsp;Appeal&nbsp;Form (Download <strong><a href="/files/2026_DHS_JAE_Appeal_Form.pdf" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">HERE</a></strong> )</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>JAE&nbsp;verification slip (showing your Junior College/Polytechnic choices)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Posting results slip (showing the Junior College/Polytechnic you have
been posted to)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>O-Level results slip</p>
</li>

<p>*Note: This&nbsp;appeal&nbsp;is not valid for DSA students. Please do
NOT submit an&nbsp;appeal&nbsp;if you already have a place in another school
under the Direct School Admission (DSA-JC).</p>
<p>Shortlisted applicants will be invited for an interview.&nbsp;Only successful
applicants will be notified. The decision by the school will be final.
