<p>Our Humanities Talent Development provides opportunities for students
who are passionate about exploring how people, processes, politics, cultures
and events are connected and interwoven across space and time. The Humanities
TD experiences will enhance students’ ability to observe, study and communicate
his/her thoughts about our human condition and our place in the world.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/tdHumanitiesAesthetics.jpg">
</div>
<p>We offer GEP special programmes such as Humanities and Social Sciences
Research Programme (<strong><a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/education-in-sg/our-programmes/nus-moe-hssr" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">HSSRP</a></strong>)
and Moot Parliament Programme (<strong><a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/education-in-sg/our-programmes/geb-special-programmes" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">MPP</a></strong>),
in-house Humanities research programmes, as well as access to other Humanities
events such as external Model United Nations (MUN) events and the NUS Geography
Challenge.</p>
<p>In Senior High (Junior College), students can look forward to taking up
H3 Humanities Options, the NUS-MOE Humanities and Social Sciences Research
(<strong><a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/education-in-sg/our-programmes/nus-moe-hssr" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">NUS-MOE HSSR</a></strong>)
as well as participating in our very own Humanities academic&nbsp;<strong>CCA</strong>,
the&nbsp;<strong>International Strategic Affairs Council</strong>&nbsp;(ISAC)
where they organise local and/or international events such as the ASEAN
Plus Summit.</p>
<p>At Dunman High, we also offer a robust Aesthetics Talent Development across
six years, with emphasis on our Music Elective Programme, and Art Special
Programme (ASP). You may visit&nbsp;<strong><a href="/academic/aesthetics-programme/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">HERE</a></strong>&nbsp;to
find out more.</p>
